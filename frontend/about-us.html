The Story

We've been in the room.

The platform began with a very ordinary pain: the gap between how polished events should feel and how fragmented the organiser's workflow usually is.

That's not a workflow. That's chaos wearing a spreadsheet.

We've seen organisers stay up until 2 AM copying participant names into certificate templates one by one. We've watched college fest coordinators send hundreds of emails manually from personal inboxes.

We've seen corporate event managers juggle a website builder, form tool, spreadsheet, design app, and mailing platform just to run a single conference. Something always falls through the cracks.

GalSol exists because event organising is painfully fragmented. We built it to collapse that chaos into one grand console: your event site, smart forms, attendee database, bulk certificates, and personalised emails working together.

One console. Every tool. Zero chaos.

Manual work becomes mapped work

Names, fields, certificate placeholders, and email variables move through one connected flow.

Five tools become one console

Website, forms, database, certificates, and mailer live under a single event workspace.

Follow-up becomes instant

Generate PDFs, store them in Drive, write links back, and dispatch emails from one pipeline.

Recognition becomes reliable

Every attendee can be seen, certified, and celebrated without the organiser losing a night of sleep.
