GalSol Cookies & Local Storage Policy

How GalSol uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform.

Last updated: May 11, 2026 5 min read
On this page
1. What We Use 2. Essential Technologies 3. Third-Party Cookies 4. Managing Your Storage 5. Contact Us

This Cookie Policy explains how GalSol uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to ensure the platform functions securely.

1 What We Use

GalSol uses Local Storage and strictly necessary cookies to ensure the platform functions securely. We do not use invasive, cross-site tracking cookies.

2 Essential Technologies

  • Authentication Tokens: We store a secure JSON Web Token (JWT) in your browser's Local Storage to maintain your session after you log in via Google OAuth.
  • Security & State: We use local storage to remember your UI preferences (like sidebar state) and temporarily cache your dashboard statistics to reduce loading times.

3 Third-Party Cookies

  • • Google OAuth: During the login process, Google will place authentication cookies on your browser. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • • Analytics: If a Creator attaches a Google Analytics (GA4) ID to their published GalSol Mini Site, Google Analytics will place cookies on the site visitor's browser to track aggregated traffic.

4 Managing Your Storage

You can clear your Local Storage and cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that clearing Local Storage will immediately log you out of the GalSol workspace.

5 Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or other technologies, please email us at: galsol.co@gmail.com.
