Workflow

From Zero to Sent
in Four Steps

01

Connect Your Google Account

Sign in with Google. Honourix requests only the permissions it needs — Gmail send access, Drive read/write, and Sheets access. You stay in full control.

02

Paste Your Sheet & Template IDs

Copy your Google Sheet ID (with participant names and emails) and your Slides template ID. No downloads, no uploads — Honourix reads them directly from your Drive.

03

Honourix Processes in the Background

For each participant, a personalized certificate PDF is generated from your Slides template, saved to a designated Drive folder, and the shareable link is written back to your Sheet.

04

Review and Send

Preview your email template with live variable substitution, review the recipient list, and hit Send. Honourix dispatches all emails via your Gmail — with certificate links embedded.
