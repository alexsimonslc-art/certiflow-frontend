Data Privacy

Your data never
leaves your Google Account

GalSol is an interface, not a data store. Every file, email, and spreadsheet stays in your Google ecosystem.

GalSol reads Sheet IDs only

We store only the Sheet ID and column mappings you configure — never the actual participant data inside your Sheet.

Emails sent from your Gmail

GalSol never sends email through its own servers. Every email is dispatched via your Gmail account using the Gmail API.

Files saved to your Drive

Generated PDFs are written directly to your designated Google Drive folder. GalSol never copies or stores them on our servers.
